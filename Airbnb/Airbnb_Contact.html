The Caledonia Urban Oasis is situated in The Village Park within the city.
By taking a bike ride you can find yourself in a beautiful Caledonia Park, Joyce Park and Glen Park Parkette pathway to forest greens.
You can also take a 5min Uber drive to one of the most prestigious malls in Toronto.
Yorkdale is a place that has very high end quality clothing and furniture.
Many tourists are delighted to walk around this mall and enjoy restaurants like The Cheesecake Factory, Pickel Barrel, Le Lert Thai Bar & Restaurant, Companiae, Earls Kitchen & Bar, MADO, Poulet Rouge, Konjiki Ramon, Stacked Pancakes & Breakfast House and Cafe Landwer Yorkdale.
Take the trains from Yorkdale to Downtown Toronto to explore the Roger's Centre, CN Tower, Ripley's Aquarium, Hockey Hall of Fame, Art Gallery of Ontario, Royal Ontario Museum and Mirvish Theatre. The Roger's Stadium is a 13min drive from the condo.
Enjoy your private suite with a Peruvian atmosphere and host! This location is fully equipped with a kitchen, bathroom master bedroom and balcony.
It has all amenities such as towels, toiletries, plates, cups, glasses, cutlery and linens. There is cooking oil, salt and pepper.
The building also has its private laundry room and 1 private parking space.
With a small fee you can leave your luggages in storage.
You can come and go as you please with a private pad lock and fob.
Therefore; checking in will never be a problem with the time of day you arrive!
